## Frequently Asked Questions

### Who is Wander for?

Wander is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. It is not designed for children or minors.

Some suggestions may involve offline activities such as walking, visiting public places, or attending a class, so users should use personal judgment and follow local rules.

### Why does Wander only give 10 suggestions per day?

Wander is intentionally limited. The daily limit prevents the app from becoming an endless feed and supports the product’s design.

### Why does Clear My Data not reset the daily limit?

Clear My Data clears favorites, completed records, and data associated with the current anonymous content identity where applicable. It does not reset the current 24-hour quota window. This prevents quota bypass.

### How do I clear favorites and completed records?

Open Wander, go to the Account or settings screen, and use Clear My Data. This clears favorites and completed records associated with the current anonymous content identity where applicable.

## 常见问题

### Wander 适合谁使用？

Wander 面向至少 18 岁以上用户，不面向儿童或未成年人。

部分锦囊可能涉及散步、前往公共场所或参加课程等线下活动，因此用户应自行判断并遵守当地规则。

### 为什么 Wander 每天只有 10 条？

Wander 是刻意限制的。每日额度避免 App 变成无限信息流，也服务于产品设计。

### 为什么清除数据后额度不重置？

「清除我的数据」会在适用情况下清除收藏、完成记录，以及当前匿名内容身份相关的数据。它不会重置当前 24 小时额度窗口。这是为了防止绕过额度限制。

### 如何清除收藏和完成记录？

打开 Wander，进入「我的」或设置页面，使用「清除我的数据」。这会在适用情况下清除当前匿名内容身份相关的收藏和完成记录。
